Product Idea: SmartPantry

Vision

To revolutionize home cooking and sustainability by intelligently managing kitchen inventory, minimizing food waste, and simplifying meal planning for households everywhere.

Users

Home Cooks: Need inspiration for meals based on ingredients they already have to avoid decision fatigue.

Budget-Conscious Shoppers: Want to avoid overbuying and ensure they use up perishables before they spoil.

Busy Families: Require quick, organized meal solutions and automated shopping lists to save time.

Key Features

Smart Inventory Tracking: Easy input via barcode scanning, receipt parsing, or voice commands.

AI Recipe Matching: Generates recipes based strictly on currently available ingredients.

Expiration Alerts: Automated push notifications when specific items are approaching their expiry date.

Waste & Savings Analytics: A dashboard showing money saved and food waste reduction over time.
